MetWind

Overview

I work with MetWind on race-specific forecasting, strategic weather analysis, and routing support for competitive sailing. The company has a long track record in high-level campaigns across Olympic classes, TP52, RC44, maxi racing, the America's Cup, and offshore projects.

My contribution is focused on turning complex model output into usable decisions: expected wind structure, local effects, shifts, pressure differences, cloud cues, and the broader strategic picture around a racecourse or offshore route.

More on the team and services: metwind.com

What I Do

  • Produce clear, race-oriented forecasts for inshore competition and offshore decision-making.
  • Support routing and strategic preparation by comparing scenarios instead of just delivering one model answer.
  • Work at the intersection of meteorology and sailing performance, where the quality of interpretation matters as much as the forecast itself.
