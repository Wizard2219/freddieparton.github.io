About

Profile

I am a performance-focused navigator working at the intersection of meteorology, oceanography, and data science.

Background

Expertise

Navigation

Over a decade of sail racing experience from dinghies to maxis inshore and offshore. I have sailed a wide variety of boats and roles and have a deep understanding of the physics behind sailing performance and strategy. I have a strong track record of success in high level campaigns, including multiple wins in many big boat classes, and a youth national championship in the Laser class in France. With my interests in meteorology, oceanography, and data science, I have focused on navigation for the last two years.

Meteorology

Master's in meteorology (MSc) from the University of Reading and two years of forecasting for teams at the highest level in the TP52, RC44, Maxi, J-Class, IRC and Olympic classes. World class daily forecasts for inshore racing and offshore routings using the best models and tools available. Often on site for key regattas to provide real-time weather support and decision making. Expert in boundary layer meteorology and a deep understanding of how weather impacts sailing performance and strategy.

Oceanography

MSci in Oceanography from the University of Southampton, with a focus on physical oceanography and ocean modeling. Experience with oceanographic data analysis, including currents, tides, and wave modeling. Understanding of how ocean conditions impact sailing performance and strategy, and how to incorporate this into navigation decisions. Research projects on deep water currents in the western Mediterranean Basin and on the correlation of El Niño events with the North Atlantic Oscillation.

Data Science

Proficient in a multitude of languages and using Python for data analysis and modeling. Experience with machine learning techniques and statistical analysis. Skilled in performance analysis and data visualization. Software development experience including building a routing algorithm and weather data pipeline. Python, Git, SQL, Jupyter, APIs, MATLAB, R, PowerBI, Excel, GIS, GOMBOC, Expedition Marine, Adrena.

Ambitions

Seeking opportunities within elite offshore and foiling campaigns including SailGP, America Cup, and Vendée Globe teams.
